👉 Keep it simple:

🧠 POSSIBLE QUESTIONS (WITH ANSWERS)
❓ Why MATLAB?

“For accurate signal processing and frequency-based encoding/decoding.”

❓ Why not real ultrasonic?

“Laptop hardware limitations above ~18kHz make it unreliable.”

❓ Is it scalable?

“Yes, the protocol layer is independent and can be extended to IoT devices.”

❓ Is it real-time?

“Yes, near real-time using backend polling.”
